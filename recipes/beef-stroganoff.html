Beef Stroganoff

Description

Beef Stroganoff is a popular dish, it's said that this dish came all the way from Russia now some might say that it used to be cooked in a different way than before but who we are to judge here ?

  • Chuck roast cut
  • Mushrooms
  • Onions
  • Pasta of your choice
  • White wine
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Beef Stock
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Worcestershire sauce

Instructions

  1. Season your beef well then in a hot pan sear your beef then remove it to another container
  2. add a bit of butter then saut√© your onions, your mushrooms follow suit
  3. add 2 table spoons of flour and mix well
  4. add 400ml of beef stock
  5. cook it for 10mins till it thickens
  6. add Worcestershire sauce, two table spoons of dijon mustard and a table spoon of sour cream
  7. finally add your beef back and let it cook on low heat for 10 mins
  8. serve it with a base of pasta of your choice
