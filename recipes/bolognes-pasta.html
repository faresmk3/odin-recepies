Bolognese pasta

Description

Bolognese pasta is an italian dish, it goes without saying a people's dish, it's simple delicious and you'll never have enough of it

Ingredients

  • Mince
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Basel
  • Garlic, Ginger(optional)
  • Bell pepper
  • Spices of your choice

    Instructions

  1. In a hot pan, drizzle some olive oil and caramelize your onions then add 4 gloves of finely chopped Garlic
  2. add your mince and don't forget to smash the mince so it doesn't clump, season it with salt and pepper
  3. add your Tomatoes and put on high heat till it reaches a boil, add some olive oil and some vinegar, turn down the heat and let it simmer for a solid 10 minutes
  4. add some oregano, your basel, smoked paprika and some ginger powder
  5. now you prepare your pasta of your choice
  6. once the sauce thickens, lift it off the heat and pour some of your sauce on your pasta and you're ready to eat
