Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a famous italian dish that uses a bolognese sauce and a white sauce combo mixed with a top layer of cheese to create the perfect dish

Ingredients

  • Mince
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Basel
  • Milk
  • Nutmeg
  • Flour
  • Butter/Olive oil
  1. Boil some water and season it well with salt, add a drizzle of olive oil to it
  2. put your lasagna into the boiling water for 4 minutes then take them out
  3. Prepare your bolognese sauce
  4. Prepare your white sauce
  5. Start by putting a thin layer of your bolognese sauce to serve as a base
  6. add a layer of lasagna
  7. add a good layer of your bolognese sauce followed by a layer of white sauce and cheese
  8. add a layer of lasagna
  9. rinse and repeat until you don't have any more space and finally dress it with a layer of cheese of your choice and voila you're done
